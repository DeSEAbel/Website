Meet the dynamic team behind the groundbreaking ocean application, Deseabel. This unlikely group of individuals, with backgrounds ranging from commerce to computer science, law to machine learning, air pollution and marine sound propagation, came together at the Ocean Hackathon 2022 in La Rochelle. Though strangers at first, these individuals quickly formed a tight-knit group, utilizing their diverse skill sets to create a winning solution. With their multidisciplinary approach, this team took home the top prize at the La Rochelle Ocean Hackathon and went on to compete at the international finale in Brest. Get ready to be amazed by the innovative solution that was born from this unlikely union of ocean enthusiasts.
